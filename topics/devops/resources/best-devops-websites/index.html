Best DevOps Websites in 2022

Home - Topics - DevOps - Resources - Best DevOps Websites in 2022

DevOps.com

Much like Reddit.com is the front page of the internet, DevOps.com is the front page of DevOps.  From the latest news, tools, methodology, practice updates, and much more DevOps.com is a great resource if you are looking for information from beginner to advanced.

DevOps.com is the largest collection of original content related to DevOps on the web and one of the top results for DevOps-related search terms. DevOps.com content includes in-depth features, bylined articles, blog posts, and breaking news about the topics that resonate with IT readers interested in DevOps: Teamwork through improved IT culture, continuous integration, automated deployment, agile development, and infrastructure-as-code among them.

Visit DevOps.com

Atlassian DevOps Blog

Atlassian is constantly pushing the boundaries on work tracking, documentation, and automation so it only makes sense that they publish a lot of helpful information in the DevOps space as well.

This blog has carpet bombed every single topic that DevOps has to offer.  From methodology, tools, monitoring, lifecycle, or any other topic you may want to investigate in your DevOps journey.

Visit The Atlassian DevOps Blog

DevOpscube.com

DevOpscube is a great website for connecting people interested in DevOps with the resources they need to be successful.  They have a bunch of self posted information along with a ton of helpful content linked out to other prominent resources as well.

DevOpscube goal is to continue to build a growing DevOps community offering the best in-depth articles, interviews, event listings, whitepapers, infographics and much more on DevOps.

Visit DevOpsCube.com

DZone DevOps

DZone is a tech site and education hub publishing articles across “Zones” of DevOps, Cloud, JavaScript, BigData, Mobile, and many others.  The DevOps portion of their website has numerous resources from self published articles to more notable DevOps engineers.  You can find real world information on tools, pratices, and process in various different industry segments.

Visit DZone.com
